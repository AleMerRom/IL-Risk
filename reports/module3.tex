\subsection{Task 3.4 --- Effective Spread from Observed Swaps (D3.6)}
\label{subsec:m3-task34}

\subsubsection{Empirical estimator}
\label{subsubsec:m3-es-formula}
For each observed swap $i$, effective spread is:
\[
ES_i = 2 D_i \frac{p^{\text{exec}}_i - p^{\text{mid}}_i}{p^{\text{mid}}_i},
\]
where $D_i=+1$ for buy and $D_i=-1$ for sell.
